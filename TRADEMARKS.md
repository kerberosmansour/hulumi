## Why Apache-2.0 only (no MIT)

Hulumi cites framework controls (CSA CCM / AICM / CAIQ, CIS Benchmarks, NIST SP 800-53 r5, NIST SP 800-218A, MITRE ATLAS) by ID only. Several of those frameworks are governed by patent / re-distribution clauses where Apache-2.0's explicit patent grant gives both upstream contributors and downstream consumers a more predictable footing than MIT's silence on patents. The single-license stance also keeps the `@hulumi/*` package metadata (`license: "Apache-2.0"`) consistent with the broader Pulumi ecosystem.
